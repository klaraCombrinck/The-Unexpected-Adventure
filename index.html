WELCOME

Hello and welcome to the Unexpected Adventure! I'm Klara, a South African with
a love of adventure-lite living and saying yes to many an unexpected thing.

"Adventure-lite"?

We all know them. The ultra-marathon runners, the triathlon-triplets and squirrel-suit junkies.
We love to watch their adventures, but when it comes to doing,
I have made my peace that I belong squarely into the self-named "adventure-lite" category.

Visit my Laid back logbook

My range of adventures have been known to include everything from a afternoon stroll to spelunking, the only constant being that they occur sporadically and they don't require extreme levels of fitness.

Stick around and I will give you a glimpse into my lightly adventure-spiced life.
